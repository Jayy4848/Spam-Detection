═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
WHY THE MODEL STRUGGLES WITH HINDI/MARATHI
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. INSUFFICIENT TRAINING DATA
   • Only 15 Hindi samples out of 75 total (20%)
   • Zero Marathi samples
   • ML models need hundreds/thousands of samples per language

2. TF-IDF LIMITATIONS
   • TF-IDF learns vocabulary from training data
   • Hindi/Marathi words not in training data = unknown tokens
   • Unknown tokens get zero weight in classification

3. KEYWORD MISMATCH
   • Phishing detector has 31 English keywords
   • Only 12 Hindi keywords (39% coverage)
   • Zero Marathi keywords (0% coverage)

4. NO LANGUAGE-SPECIFIC PREPROCESSING
   • No stemming/lemmatization for Hindi/Marathi
   • No handling of Devanagari script variations
   • No transliteration support (Devanagari ↔ Roman)

5. RULE-BASED FALLBACK IS ENGLISH-ONLY
   • All fallback keywords are English
   • Hindi/Marathi messages fall through to "personal" category
